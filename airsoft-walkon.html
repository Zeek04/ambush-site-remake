Airsoft Walk-on Events

Day & Nites

Our Airsoft Walk-On Events are designed to accommodate individuals and small groups of all skill levels. Ambush has earned an outstanding reputation for combining Airsoft enthusiasts with first time players! All players are combined then split into 2 teams.

Walk-On Events do not require a reservation or deposit. However if a gun rental is needed, reserve one here!
