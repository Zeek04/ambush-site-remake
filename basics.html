BASICS

Thank you for checking us out. Whether you are planning a group outing, a one-time visit, or need information; we are here to help! Ambush is a premier outdoor Airsoft and Paintball facility located in Conestoga, PA. We take pride in being the best and the safest on the East Coast!! Here are a few basics to help you decide what is best for your visit and provide general information about our facility.

Walk on Events:

We schedule specific Walk-On Events throughout each month (March - December), where you can play without a reservation or specific number of players.

Walk-On Events are great for any person playing by themselves or in a small group and have not reserved to play privately.

General Information

  • We play rain or shine!
  • All players are required to sign a release of liability waiver before they play.
  • Any player 10 - 17 years old must have a parent/guardian signature on their completed waiver.
  • At the field: we accept cash and credit card.
  • The minimum age to play is 10 years old!
  • Any non-player is welcome to watch games, at no cost!
  • You can bring your own food and drinks (nonalcoholic).
  • We have a safe-area conveniently located near our playing fields.
  • Our safe-area seats nearly 400 people consisting of shaded picnic tables.
  • We provide 60 lock boxes to secure your gear/supplies while you are on the playing field.
  • We are open year-round, with heated facilities for players in-between games.
  • No alcohol allowed on premises.
  • Gift certificates are available.
  • Drive slowly in and out of driveway. Speed Limit is 10 MPH
