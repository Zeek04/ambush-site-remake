General Paintball Information

Who can play?

Any person 10 years of age or older is welcome to play at Ambush

Release of Liability Waiver:

Each person will need to complete a Paintball Waiver before playing.
Any player under the age of 18 must have a parent/guardian signature on their completed waiver.
A new waiver is needed each time you play.

Safety Briefing:

Ambush believes safety is the key to having fun. Before you play, we will inform you of our rules and regulations, on and off of the fields.
If you are playing on a Walk-On Day, the main briefing is at 8:30am.
If you are attending a private booking, it will be at the beginning of your reservation time.

Protection:

Gun Safety - all guns must have a gun plug or sleeve and must be disarmed while in the Safe Zone.
Face Wear - a full face mask must be worn at all times outside of the Safe Zone. masks are available to rent.

Paintballs:

Ambush paintballs only.

Chronograph:

You are welcome to bring your own gun anytime you come to play. It must be chronographed each visit by an Ambush personnel.
