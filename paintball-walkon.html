Paintball Walk-on Events

Day & Nites

Our Paintball Walk-On Events are designed to accommodate individuals and small groups of all skill levels. Ambush has earned an outstanding reputation for combining paintball enthusiasts with first time players!
Walk-On Events do not require a reservation or deposit. However, please contact us if you have a significant size group (10 or more players). All players are combined then split into 2 teams.
