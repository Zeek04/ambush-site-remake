Private Paintball Reservation

Thank you for choosing Ambush and for being a group organizer. All the information you need to organize your group is below.
We look forward to hearing from you.

1.How would you like to play:

If you would like to play privately, continue below.
If you would like to play with others check out our Paintball Walk-On Events

2.Private Booking:

We offer 2 packages: 3 Hour Package or All Day Package.

3 Hour Package

Time Options: 8:30am-11:30am OR 12pm-3pm
$450 includes 10 players with guns, masks, pod packs and 4000 paintballs
Additional players are $20 per player includes: gun, mask and pod pack.
Reservation and deposit are required.

All Day Package

Pay per player includes: gun, mask and 500 rounds of paintballs
$55 per player with 15 - 19 players
$52 per player with 20 - 29 players
$49 per player with 30 - 39 players
$45 per player with +40 players
$5 off per player not needing a rental gun. We must be notified via email 2 weeks in advance of rental number.
must have at least 15 players
Reservation and deposits are required.

3.Making A reservation:

Sumbit your Date Request Form

4.Secure Your Reservation:

Deposit(s) are required to secure your reservation. Once your reservation is confirmed, you will be given instructions to submit your deposit(s). Any deposit received will be applied to your package on the day of your reservation.

All Day Package only:

An additional deposit of $25 per player minus your $100 deposit. This allows us to prepare accordingly for your group.
We recommend you collect $25 from each player for a sense of commitment from them.
Remaining costs are due on game day.
Example of Deposits:
15 Players X $25 = $375 minus $100 (deposit) = $275 final deposit.

Reservation Cancellation/Reschedule Policy:

We hope this doesn't happen but we do understand that you may have to cancel your reservation due to uncontrollable circumstances.
Once a reservation is confirmed, please notify us, via email, about cancelling or rescheduling.
To receive a full refund or to transfer your deposit to another date, we must be notified 8 days prior to your reservation date.
Any cancellation or reschedule request made within a week of your reservation date results in no refund to particial refund depending on package:
3 Hour Package reservation - no refund
All Day Package reservation - final deposit refund only.
Within 3 days or less of reservation date, no refund and no transfer.
In the event of "No Show", your deposit is nonrefundable and nontransferable.

General Information:

We play rain or shine.
-severe weather causing closing, we will be in contact with you.
All players must complete a Paintball Waiver(available at field as well).
Any player 10 - 17 years old, must have a parent/guardian signature on their completed waiver.
Arrive 20 minutes prior to your reservation time; to complete waivers, gather at your designated table(s) and prepare for rules.
